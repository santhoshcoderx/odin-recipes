Slow Cooked Barbecue Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce, divided
  • 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper on all sides. Place in a slow cooker; pour 1/4 cup barbecue sauce and vinegar on top.
  2. Cook on Low for 3 hours.
  3. Combine brown sugar, onion powder, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes in a small bowl.
  4. Pour out liquid from the slow cooker and add remaining barbecue sauce. Sprinkle chicken with brown sugar-spice mixture.
  5. Cook on Low, stirring occasionally, until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 3 more hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
